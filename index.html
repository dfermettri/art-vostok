ARTVOSTOK

We create a wide range of digital stylized and realistic art content from 2D sketches to highly detailed, fully rigged, and animated 3D assets for all types of projects.

Artvostok is a game art studio, established in 2006. We strive for innovation, always working to push the boundaries of our art to provide the best quality through creative Collaboration with our partners. Our strong management team ensures high quality and well-coordinated work. Our main goal is to have a strong and long-lasting relationship with our partners. Our company is continuously adapting and improving the team's knowledge is an important factor for us. So our employees attend a variety of events, help to teach students, organize meetups, and host lectures.
